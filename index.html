WhatsApp (PDF bill)
1. Open WhatsApp → open the chat with the bill
2. Tap the PDF file to open it
3. Tap the ⋮ menu (top-right) → "Save to Downloads"
4. Come back here → tap 📁 File/PDF tab → select from Downloads
💬
WhatsApp (Bill image / screenshot)
1. Open the bill image in WhatsApp
2. Tap the download ↓ icon → saved to Gallery
3. Come back here → 📷 Camera tab → From Gallery
📧
Gmail (PDF attachment)
1. Open Gmail → open the bill email
2. Tap the PDF attachment → tap Download ↓ or ⋮ → Save to Files
3. Come back here → 📁 File/PDF tab → select from Downloads
